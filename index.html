Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh fosters creativity through technology, research, and emerging industries

Sustainability

Promoting green energy, reducing pollution, and supporting eco-friendly practices.

Infrastructure

Modernizing transport, energy, and housing for economic growth and resilience.

Empowerment

Encouraging women's rights, education, and entrepreneurship for societal progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Each of us has an unlimited amount of creative capacity. We have the ability to change the world and make it better. The challenge is not capability; it is in the approach and mindset we choose. Poverty is not caused by people; it is created by the system we have built. If we design a system that helps people flourish rather than trap them, we will eliminate poverty from the world. This is why I believe that business, when done right, can solve social problems and unleash human potential for good."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
